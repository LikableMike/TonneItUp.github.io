Introduction

The TonneItUp application allows users to create an account/login and connect with their personal trainer. Features include scheduling appointments, chatting with the trainer, tracking workout progress, and more, helping users stay consistent and committed to their goals.

Features and Functionality

Quick Guide

1. Users will sign up within the app and input introductory information such as height and weight. Using these values, their BMI is determined, which is tracked as a progress graph on the home page. More information may be requested by the personal trainer.
2. Users may schedule appointments with Makayla through the application and chat with her when needed. Appointments may be recurring or on one time basis.
3. Makayla will chat with the client and based on her expertise and her clientâ€™s goals; she will create a tailored workout plan and diet if needed. The clients may access the workout plan through the app, as well as every new workout plan given to them by Makayla.
4. Through using the application over time, clients can track their progress by reporting weight changes at consistent intervals.
5. Users will receive notifications about upcoming appointments, new workout plans, and progress milestones in order to keep them updated and engaged throughout their fitness journey.

Account Creation

The account creation process starts with the main page. It showcases two buttons, Signup and Login. Depending on if you are a returning user or a new user, users can select the desired option.

Authentication

All accounts are authenticated through email verification for security and access to the app's features.

Homepage Options

Access tools like scheduling, progress tracking, chat, workout plans, and more from the homepage.

Progress Tracking

1. Visual Charts: The page includes dynamic charts or graphs that visually display changes in weight over time, helping users identify trends and patterns.
2. Historical Data: Users can view a detailed history of past entries, allowing them to track long-term programs and evaluate the effectiveness of their workout routines.

Appointment Page

Change profile details like username, password, email, and phone number easily through the settings page.

Workout Page

The workout page is where you will view the workouts assigned by your personal trainer. Clicking into them will show you details relating to the exercises within them.

Settings Page

Change profile details like username, password, email, and phone number easily through the settings page.
